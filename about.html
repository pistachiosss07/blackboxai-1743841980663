Our Story

Shop Sarisari was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to connect customers with unique, handcrafted products from talented artisans around the world.

Our Journey

2015

Founded in Manila

Started as a small boutique showcasing local Filipino crafts

2017

Expanded Online

Launched our e-commerce platform to reach customers worldwide

2019

Global Artisans

Partnered with craftspeople from 15 different countries

2023

New Showroom

Opened our flagship store in Makati with over 200 artisans represented
